Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Fettuccine Alfredo is quick and easy and super tasty. If you want to add some more heartiness to the meal, you can add cooked chicken to the meal and change it up by adding some steamed broccoli.

Ingredients

  • 1 package fettuccine pasta.
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • About 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt, Pepper, and garlic to taste
  • Romano and Parmeasan cheeses

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. (or to your liking)
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese.
  3. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce.
